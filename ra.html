What is this law?


RA 10586 or the Anti-Drunk and Drugged Driving Act of 2013 is a law that was written/created by Senator Vicente C. Sotto III and it states that "the State shall penalize the acts of driving under the influence of alcohol, dangerous drugs and other intoxicating substances and shall inculcate the standards of safe driving and the benefits that may be derived from it through institutional programs and appropriate public information strategies". This means the law will punish anyone who is caught driving while not being mentally safe to do so.
