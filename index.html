Introduction

Gluttony


Our assigned folly is Gluttony. Before anything else, we'd like to explain what gluttony means. According to Google, "gluttony is a habitual greed or excess in eating." while this definition is true, gluttony, one of the 7 Deadly Sins, can also mean an unhealthy obsession of something even when it is not exactly a need anymore. People who experience gluttony are commonly people who experience greed because of their unreasonable want for something they can eat.
