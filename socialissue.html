What is this social issue?


Drunk driving is when someone drives a vehicle under the influence of alcohol, dangerous drugs, or other similar substances, but in this topic, we'll focus more on alcohol since it is the most common. When the driver intakes alcohol or other beverages that makes a person incapable of driving a vehicle, there is a big possibility of that person endangering the life of others or his own. When a person drinks alcohol, the mind becomes dizzy and the person is not able to think or act normally, therefore causing that person to be unable to focus on driving and possibly lead to misfortunes.

How is this related to Gluttony?

As we already know, gulttony is a habitual greed or excess in eating or drinking. People have the freedom to buy and drink alcohol in any time they want as long as theyre above the age of 18 years old. Anyone (except minors) can drink alcohol anywhere after they drive or if they're not planning on driving any time soon. Even with all this time, people still choose to drink during or right before driving. This is related to gluttony as this is an unhealthy obssession of a drink which can easily be accessed and consumed in less dangerous times.

What can you do to avoid this?


In order for you, the reader, to avoid such mistakes/accidents, we are going to tell you things you can do to avoid this.


  1. Always choose someone who does not drink often to drive

    2. People who don't drink often tend to have more control and are more responsible than people who drink a lot. This means that these people can be trusted in driving a vehicle when it comes to being drunk or not.


  2. When going out alone, never get alcoholic drinks, only water or soda.

    3. When going out alone, there is noone who can drive for you or even help you if you get drunk. If you don't have anyone with you, nobody is there to take care of you when you want to go home or in case something happens. It's always better to just avoid getting drunk altogether in order to avoid any risk.


  3. If you plan on drinking, please do so responsibly.

    4. When you really want/need to, then please be responsible and know exactly when and where to drink. It's important to control ourselves and only drink heavy drinks when you are sure you won't be doing anything big and difficult like driving. Better yet, only drink small amounts of alcohol in order to avoid getting drunk.


  4. Never get in a vehicle with a drunk driver

    5. If you know that the driver of a vehicle is drunk, its better to just avoid them and not get in the car with them. You can never predict when a person makes a mistake. Much more when that person is drunk. If the drunk driver is your friend or a person you know, tell them to get out of the vehicle and wait until they're feeling better and is okay to drive a vehicle.


Group's Reflection

People have been warned countlessly already about the dangers of driving when not feeling too well. For some reason, after drinking alcohol, people still force themselves to drive and risk people getting hurt, including themselves. Drunk drivers have gotten into accidents, injuries, or car crashes that have spilt the red blood of the innocent that don’t even know what was happening until they get affected by the accident.

Wine and alcohol have been known to be present even during the time of the Greeks where there were parties, celebrations, important events that have beer and wine. These 2 beverages have been known to affect Zeus, God of the Skies and Lightning’s decisions and actions. The decisions the god made when he was drunk where nothing short of terrible and irresponsible choices.

In the Philippines, though not too frequently, still experience car accidents which include the driver not being mentally stable due to intake of alcohol. According to Acta Medica Philippina, 12,000 road users die annually due to road crashes and 1/3 of them are due to alcohol-related issues. This is a scary amount, and the number of deaths will only keep getting bigger unless we do something about it.
